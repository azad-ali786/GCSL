About Us

Green Communication Systems Lab (GCSL) focuses on designing sustainable, energy-efficient, energy-aware, and environmentally aware communications and networking. The lab promotes innovations, new technologies, concepts, and principles toward a viable information and communications technology (ICT). The people in the lab work in physical, medium access control (MAC) and network layers of following area of interest.

  • MIMO Communication
  • Cognitive Radios
  • Unmanned Area Vehicle (UAV)
  • Underwater Acoustic Communications
  • Beyond 5G (B5G) Networks
  • Free Space Optical Communications
  • OFDM Systems
  • Device-to-Device (D2D) Communications
  • Energy Harvesting in Wireless Communications
  • Wireless Power Transfer
  • Power Line Communications (PLCs)
